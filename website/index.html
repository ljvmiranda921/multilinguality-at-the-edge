Multilinguality at the Edge

Developing Language Models for the Global South

Lester James V. Miranda, Songbo Hu, Roi Reichart, Anna Korhonen

code · paper

The Problem: Communities with the largest linguistic diversity are also the most limited by compute.

[ figure loads here ]

The field has several names for this: the low-resource double bind, the square-one bias, Zeno's paradox of language technology, among others.

The Challenge: How can we build language models that cater to this linguistic diversity while having a small footprint?

Our Approach: We survey 232 papers across multilinguality and efficient NLP to understand the state of the art and the challenges of combining the two.
